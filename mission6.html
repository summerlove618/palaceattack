Explanation
The chimneys of Jagyeongjeon Hall decorated with the 10 signs of longevity (Treasure No. 810).

History

Jagyeongjeon Hall, located to the east of the living quarters, was built for Queen Sinjeong. She was the adoptive mother of young King Gojong. Jagyeongjeon Hall was built when gyeongbokgung Palace was rebuilt by the Daewongun, the ambitious father of King Gojong.
